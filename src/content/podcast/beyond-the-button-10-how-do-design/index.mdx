This month, Michelle is joined by two new faces: [Tim Legierse](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tim-legierse-40663aa4/), one of the most tenured members of the zeroheight team, and [Beau Ulrey](https://www.linkedin.com/in/beauulrey/), Sr. Design System Manager at U.S. Bank. This month’s discussion is about how design systems can foster user trust. Sure, a good set of reusable components can create a more consistent product. But what does that mean for users? According to the folks at U.S. Bank, a more consistent UI is a key driver for user trust and adoption, and they’ll tell us how.
