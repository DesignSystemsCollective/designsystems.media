**Guest**

Adekunle Oduye (Add-eh-koon-lay Oh-due-yay) is currently building design systems at [Plaid](https://plaid.com/). Outside of work, he is a coach/mentor, conference speaker and co-host of the [Code and Pixels](https://www.codeandpixels.fm/) podcast. 

You can learn more about him on his website ([https://www.adekunleoduye.com](https://www.adekunleoduye.com/)), linkedin ([https://www.linkedin.com/in/adekunleoduye](https://www.linkedin.com/in/adekunleoduye)), or twitter ([https://twitter.com/adekunleoduye](https://twitter.com/adekunleoduye)).
