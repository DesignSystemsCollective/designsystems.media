Mike and Chris discuss design systems at the enterprise level, the history of systems, and UX design at the beginning of the internet. 

_Guest:_ Mike Bowser is the Design Director, Design Systems at Vista. He is a UX/UI designer, design team leader, and educator with expertise in design systems, UX research, and UI design. His recent focus is closing the gap between how we concept and design and how products are actually built. He’s also interested in the growing opportunities for automation in the future of UX design and design systems.

You can find Mike on Twitter as @[mikebowser](http://twitter.com/mikebowser) and on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mikebowser/).
