Chris and Micah discuss creating a singular design system that scales to support a massive app ecosystem, using tokens to align design across disparate products, and transitioning from individual teams working on many products to one company working on a library of products.  
  
Guest:  
  
Micah Godbolt is a design systems advocate, speaker, trainer, and writer. Micah is a software engineer for [Fluent UI](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/fluentui#/) at Microsoft and author of [Frontend Architecture for Design Systems](http://shop.oreilly.com/product/0636920040156.do) published by O’Reilly Media. You can find Micah at [micahgodbolt.com](https://www.micahgodbolt.com/), on Twitter as [@micahgodbolt](https://twitter.com/micahgodbolt), and on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/micahgodbolt/).

You can find Chris on Twitter as [@chrisstrahl](https://twitter.com/chrisstrahl) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/chrisstrahl/).
