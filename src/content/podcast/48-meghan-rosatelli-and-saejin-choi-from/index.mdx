Meghan Rosatelli and Saejin Choi join Chris to discuss transitioning multiple design systems into one, shifting system consumers’ mindset from working with a local system to working with a global system, and using play to drive excitement and adoption of a design system.  
  
Guest: Meghan Rosatelli is the Director of Design for Capital One’s enterprise design system. She also teaches courses in digital arts at the University of Richmond. You can find Meghan on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/meghanrosatelli/).  
  
Guest: Saejin is currently the Experience Lead for Capital One's design system. His previous life at Capital One was leading the Digital Payments and Commerce design team. You can find Saejin on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/saejin/), or via his years-long dormant Twitter account [@saejinchoi](https://twitter.com/saejinchoi).
