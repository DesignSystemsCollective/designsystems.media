Morgane and Chris discuss threading design into the fabric of how business works, capturing a wide range of complexity needs within your design system, moving away from traditional design tools to providing a playground that enables anyone to design with code in the browser, lessons from game development and how they apply to design systems.  
  
Guest:  
  
Morgane Peng is Design Director at [Societe Generale](https://www.societegenerale.com/) Corporate & Investment Banking, illustrator, speaker, and indie game developer at [Koruldia](https://www.koruldia.com/). You can find Morgane at [morganepeng.com](https://www.morganepeng.com/), on Twitter as [@morganepeng](https://twitter.com/morganepeng), on [Medium](https://medium.com/@morganepeng), and on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/morganepeng/).
