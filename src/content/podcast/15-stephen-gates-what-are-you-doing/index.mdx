Stephen Gates (currently at InVision; formerly at Citi and Starwood) and Chris discuss advice for leaders needing to drive adoption and success of their design systems, how to assess and foster trust between design system creators and consumers, treating design systems as infrastructure, and the Amazing Design People List to help designers find work during COVID-19.  
  
Guest:  
  
Stephen Gates is Head Design Evangelist at InVision, host of [The Crazy One podcast](http://thecrazy1.com/), designer, and international keynote speaker. You can find Stephen on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/sdgates) or [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/sdgates/) as @sdgates and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/stepdgat/).

Chris Strahl is co-founder and CEO of [Knapsack](https://www.knapsack.cloud/), host of [@TheDSPod](https://twitter.com/thedspod), DnD DM, and occasional river guide. You can find Chris on Twitter as [@chrisstrahl](https://twitter.com/chrisstrahl) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/chrisstrahl/).
