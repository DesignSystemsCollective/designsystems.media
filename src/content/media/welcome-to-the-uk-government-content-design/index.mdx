This video is an introduction to the UK government content design community. It is for content designers across government and the public sector who may be new to government, or interested in joining the Civil Service. 

In the video, you will hear content designers from across government explain how being in the community has improved their work, how the content design profession is developing and ways that the community helps people meet and learn together. 

To find out more about about the content community and how to join email content-community@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk
