Meet our guests:

Sarah Hamdi, https://sarahhamdi.com/
Sarah is a Senior Software Developer at Wealthsimple. She's worked in tech since 2016 and in the nonprofit/immigrant and refugee serving sector before that. She will happily chat with you about drawing, sewing, and comics.

Sarah's talk: Measuring Design System Adoption
Have you ever tried to prove usage of your Design System? Need to build trust with your engineering leadership that your Design System was worth the investment? This talk will cover how to track components usage across your codebase, why it's important to do so, and how this effort makes your work easier as a team.

---

Kristen, https://twitter.com/ackristen
Kristen is a Product Designer based in Toronto. Formally trained as an Industrial Designer, her practice is rooted in systems thinking, playfulness, curiosity and a deep care for the people she builds for. Offline you can find her gardening, biking, hiking and everything in between.

Kristen's talk: Building a design system for a creative audience.
This talk will focus on designing for flexibility, creative freedom and redefining what consistent means on websites.
