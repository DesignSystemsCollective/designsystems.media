Making a claim to an employment tribunal is now easier with a new digital service on GOV.UK. 

This is a last resort for people who feel they've been wronged by an employer. The new digital service is a much simpler alternative to making a paper claim. 

See the live service for yourself at: 
https://www.gov.uk/apply-employment-tribunal
