To view your driving licence online, go to www.gov.uk/view-driving-licence

You’ll be able to see if you have any points on your licence, check what types of vehicles you can drive and generate a check code to share your licence information with other people.

To view your driving licence online, you’ll need:
- your driving licence number
- your National Insurance number
- the postcode on your driving licence

 You can also get a check code by calling the DVLA on 0300 083 0013. Lines are open:
- Monday to Friday, 8am to 7pm
- Saturday, 8am to 2pm
