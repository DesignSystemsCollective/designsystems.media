Not all vibe-coding platforms are built for the same job. The best tool depends on what you're building and where you're building it—not what's trending.

Quick Guide:
🔹 Technical teams working in existing codebases → Cursor or Claude
🔹 Rapid prototyping new projects from scratch → Lovable or v0
🔹 Evolving products with established design systems → Supernova

The Key Insight:
In established companies, the challenge isn't generating something that looks good. It's generating something that actually fits your real product context. The moment you have to say "ignore the UI, focus on the idea," you've already lost significant value.

Choose your platform based on your environment, your design system, and your team's needs and not the hype cycle.
