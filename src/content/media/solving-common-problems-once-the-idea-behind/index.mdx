We are moving towards towards a fundamentally digital government - one in which we share code, patterns, platforms, components and best practice for approaching common technological and design problems across government. 

People at the heart of government digital transformation talk about how building reusable platforms like GOV.UK Verify, GOV.UK Pay and GOV.UK Notify will transform government digital services.
