Talk #1: Defining, Strategizing & Starting Your Design System
Laura Schaefer, Sr Manager UX Strategy

You need a design system, but how do you start? Learn how to define your audience, first tasks and goals to create a plan you can socialize and deliver on.

About Laura
Laura is a Design Leader with 10+ years experience in Digital Design across Agency and Enterprise shops, working in Visual Design, User Experience Design, Product, Strategy (or any combination of those roles). She's particularly obsessed with Design Systems, Design Operations and creating consistent, effective brand experiences across multiple devices & platforms. She also uses her fondness of planning and systems into her love of quilting during her spare time.

Talk #2: Scaling Design Systems, inclusively
Eduardo Alarcon, Director, Global Digital Design Systems, Scotiabank

Canvas, Scotiabank’s Global Design System, is turning 3 years old and it’s now being used in more than 5 countries. How are we scaling it in an inclusive way? Let’s talk about the framework we’re using to design winning strategies, and some of the lessons we’ve learned along the way

About Eduardo
Eduardo is the Director of Global Design Systems at Scotiabank. His purpose is to help transform the world into a happier place for everyone, and that’s why he’s passionate about unlocking the exponential power Design Systems have, to enable organizations in building world-class digital experiences that everyone loves. Eduardo moved from Peru early this year and is constantly playing with local ingredients to continue learning how to cook delicious Peruvian food that also brings joyful moments to the table.
