GOV⁠.⁠UK Forms provides a quick and easy way to create online forms replacing the need for PDF’s and document based forms. In just a matter of minutes you can create a new online form that’s quick to process and focused on accessibility, making it even easier for people to complete. Best of all, there’s no need for any technical knowledge and it’s completely free!
If you work in central government find out how you can register for an account and start building forms now at: https://www.forms.service.gov.uk/get-started
