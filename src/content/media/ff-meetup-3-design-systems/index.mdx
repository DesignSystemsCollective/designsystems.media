In this stream, Varya Stepanova and Irina Illustrova will present a design systems related talk under the topic of "From Survival to Growth: Enhancing Design Systems via Seamless Collaboration".

Description: This talk explores how effective collaboration can transform design systems from basic tools into vital products within organizations. Irina and Varya are sharing insights from their research and experience, and offer practical strategies for enhancing collaboration with stakeholders and across teams. They stress the importance of treating the design system as a product in its own right. The presentation is packed with real-world examples, practical advice, and innovative ideas.
