The internet had transformed the way we live.

Assisted Digital is the term used to describe a range of developments, strategies, and actions to ensure that no one is left behind. 

The approach is to get people online and share the benefits of doing so. Assisted Digital enable more people get online to access public services which are more conveniently accessible, easier to use, and therefore the preferred choice of service users.
