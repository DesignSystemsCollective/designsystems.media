Config 2024: Design systems for XL displays (June Lee & Dale Sande, Alaska Airlines)

Join June and Dale on a journey into design systems as they delve into the creation of a design system for XL digital displays. Alaska Airlines is currently enhancing the airport experience, including at the San Francisco International Airport. Their design system team, internal systems team, and marketing counterparts are pioneering digital signage designs that embody Alaska's brand. The mission? To craft a robust system that is flexible, scalable, and adaptable to a diverse range of screen sizes and devices.

Featuring: June Lee, Product Designer, Alaska Airlines & Dale Sande, Principal Engineer, Alaska Airlines
