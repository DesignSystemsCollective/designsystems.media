Design Better co-hosts Aarron Walter and Eli Woolery chat with design systems expert Dan Mall at this online event September 21, 2023. Dan took questions from the audience about how design systems are created, maintained, and implemented in large scale organizations. 

Learn more at http://designbetterpodcast.com

About Dan Mall
Dan Mall has a background in helping companies ranging from Eventbrite, to Nike, to United Airlines, develop and deploy sustainable design systems. Now he’s trying to share as much of what he’s learned to help designers get the respect they deserve. Join us for an AMA where you can ask him about your own design system challenges, learn more about his new courses at Design System University, and more.
