Dave Rupert and Chris Coyier welcome Brad and Ian Frost to discuss the launch of their new design tokens course. The conversation explores the importance of design systems, the challenges of implementing design tokens, and the need for collaboration between designers and developers. They share insights from their travels, cultural observations, and the evolution of design practices in the digital landscape.
