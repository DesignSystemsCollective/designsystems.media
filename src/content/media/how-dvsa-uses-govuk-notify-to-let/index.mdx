The Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA) has used GOV.UK Notify to remind people when their MOT is due. With 28% of MOTs performed late, the DVSA needed an easy way to send reminders. 

GOV.UK Notify is a system that lets you send emails, text messages and letters to your users. 

In this video we talk to Lauren Rousseau and Neil Barlow from DVSA on how GOV.UK Notify provided an free and simple solution for them. 

Read more on https://www.notifications.service.gov.uk/
