What's Inside  
In this session, we explored the future of design tokens with industry leaders, covering:
→ What design tokens are and why they matter  
→ The importance of standardization and the challenges of token bloat  
→ Real-world examples and best practices for token implementation  
→ How to keep documentation and libraries up to date  
→ The impact of AI on design systems  
→ Tools and workflows shaping the industry (Infa Tokens, Style Dictionary, Figma Variables, and more)  
→ Fostering collaboration within teams and communities
