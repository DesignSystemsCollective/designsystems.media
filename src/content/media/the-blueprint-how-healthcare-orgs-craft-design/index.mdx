Join design system experts Cameron Roberson (Manager, User Experience at Blue Cross Blue Shield Louisiana), Krizia Fernando (Lead Designer, Design Systems at Evernorth), and Lee Wells (Associate Director, Enterprise Design Standards at Humana). 

They'll discuss how healthcare organizations craft and leverage design systems, including:

- Structure of their design systems and its primary beneficiaries
- Unique approach healthcare takes to design systems and the key role accessibility plays
- Influence of laws and regulations on the design and adoption of these systems
