Kat Stevens is a senior developer who previously worked at start-ups and now works in the centre of government, at the Government Digital Service (GDS).

This video is for anyone who is interested in knowing how to get a job as a senior developer - and what life is like at GDS. In it, tech leader Kat explains what is expected of senior developers and some of the key skills you need to succeed including:

- Being able to absorb different information

- Make effective decisions so your team is solving the right problems

- Keeping a clear focus on user needs
