​From Linting to Decisions

​AI tools speed up execution, but they also introduce a lot of small, silent decisions that end up shaping our design systems. I'll walk through a prototype I built with Claude and Cursor that treats those moments as decisions instead of deviations and captures them in real time. The idea is to explore how AI can help us make and own decisions, not just execute them faster.

Level: Beginner to intermediate. Accessible, no heavy AI or engineering background needed.

​Teaching Claude Code to Speak Figma

​Learn the structure and possibilities of complex agent skills and how to chain them into pipelines. Demos using some custom plugins, the Figma-CLI, and the native Figma MCP running diferent kind of tasks from generation on Figma, code and auditing your Figma to code parity.

​Level: intermediate, pro
