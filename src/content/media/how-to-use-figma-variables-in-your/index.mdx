Supernova brought to you a webinar featuring Raquel Pereira (Friends of Figma Community Advocate and DesignOps at Volkswagen Digital Solutions) and Beatriz Novais (Design System Advocate at Supernova) talking about the fundamentals of Figma variables and design tokens.

The webinar includes a live demonstration showing how to create Figma variables and connect them to Supernova through the Supernova plugin.
