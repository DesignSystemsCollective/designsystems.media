In this Design System talk, Amy Hupe takes you through How To Maintain A Design System with the aim of providing UI designers the design system best practices to maintain an effective design system.

Design System Talk Abstract:

During her eight years of working on design systems, Amy has learned a thing or two about what causes a design system to fail and how to maintain a design system. In this Design System talk, she shares the most common causes of a poor design system’s demise and provides tips on how to avoid them and how to maintain a design system.

Amy Hupe 

Amy is a UK-based design systems and content design consultant. Over the past 8 years, she's worked on design systems for some of the biggest names around, including GOV.UK, BT, Springer Nature, Babylon Health and the Wellcome Trust.

Whether she’s leading systems work or designing content, Amy works to champion simplicity and inclusion in the face of increasing complexity.
