A recap of The Question Episode 060 with co host Nathan Curtis on where AI is failing us as design system practitioners. 

We settled on this idea: until an AI model can guarantee the promises your design system makes, AI is best treated as a collaborator that scaffolds, accelerates, and proposes—not a factory that ships.
