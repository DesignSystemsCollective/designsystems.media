Imagine you could call upon a superhero to accelerate product development, elevate user experience, and unite teams. Mark's talk introduces you to the untapped superpowers of design systems, and all you have to do is lean in.

Mark is a Director of Product Design at InVision and co-hosts the UX, Coffee + Code podcast. With a passion for design systems, and a background in design and engineering, he is known for connecting cross-discipline teams around the world to scale the reach and impact of design.
