As the Valuation Office Agency values 1.97 million non-domestic properties, it has to make contact with rate-payers. The Agency used GOV.UK Notify to send out 150,000 emails about the re-evaluation. 

GOV.UK Notify is a system that lets you send emails, text messages and letters to your users. 

In this video we talk to Paul Philips and Meera Nayyar from the Valuation Office Agency on how they used GOV.UK Notify to digitise a paper process.
