GOV.UK launched in 2012. It replaced nearly 2,000 government websites with just one. Since 2012, GOV.UK has been viewed more than 14 billion times. It gets an average 3.6 million visits a day. It has won awards and it has influenced governments all over the world from Australia to Israel.

Find out more about GOV.UK, the place to find simpler, clearer, faster government information and services online https://www.gov.uk/
