​As 2023 draws to a close, design system teams are starting to put plans in place for what they’ll be tackling in the new year. But if you haven’t done this kind of planning before, you might fall victim to one of the biggest pitfalls: simply adding more components to work on.

​Design System University founder Dan Mall has helped hundreds of design system teams make strategic plans for their design systems that make significant impact for their organizations. In this free webinar, Dan shares his top tips for making decisions that move your design system forward.
