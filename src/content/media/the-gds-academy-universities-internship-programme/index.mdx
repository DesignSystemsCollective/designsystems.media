In 2017, GDS Academy, DWP and HMRC partnered to run an internship programme with 3 universities. 

Students from University of Leeds, University of Manchester and Manchester Metropolitan University attended the 8-week Universities Internship Programme, designed to develop digital skills and showcase the Civil Service as a career option.
