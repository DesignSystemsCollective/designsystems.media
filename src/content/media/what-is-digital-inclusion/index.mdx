As government services become digital by default, we must design them to be accessible and work well for as many of these users as we can.

Assisted Digital is the term used to describe a range of developments, strategies, and actions aimed at ensuring that no one is left behind. 

Find out more about accessibility across government on the GOV.UK Accessibility blog:
