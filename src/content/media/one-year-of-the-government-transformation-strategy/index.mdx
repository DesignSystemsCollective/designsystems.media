In 2017, the Government Transformation Strategy was published.  The Strategy sets out how government will use digital to transform the relationship between the citizen and state.

Michelle Thorp, Managing Director of Digital, Technology and Estates, and Dylan Thomas, Director, Technology and Smart Cities, International Trade and Investment at the Department for International Trade talk about what transformation means for the Department for International Trade.
