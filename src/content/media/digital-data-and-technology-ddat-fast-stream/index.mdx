Fast Streamers across the UK government talk about their experiences on the Digital, Data and Technology (DDaT) Fast Stream. 

The 4 year leadership programme is available to graduates and existing civil servants. If you are a civil servant, you don't need a degree.

Applications for the 2019 recruitment window are open from 26 September to 24 October 2019.
