Supernova hosted an engaging Fireside Chat that featured the remarkable Patrycja Radaczynska, an advocate in the realm of Design Systems.

In this insightful discussion, she delved into her journey, which began as a passionate project focused on design systems and eventually led her to become an integral part of Babbel's dedicated team. During the conversation, Patrycja also generously shared her wisdom on the intricacies of developing a design system at Babbel, as well as strategies for fostering alignment among stakeholders and enhancing collaboration between designers and developers.
