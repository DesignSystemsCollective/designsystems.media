Claude Code knows your codebase but locks out designers. v0 lets everyone in but ships a generic UI. Supernova Portal is what happens when you don't have to compromise.

Supernova Portal - AI prototyping grounded in your real codebase and design system, so anyone on your team can build high-fidelity prototypes that actually look and work like your product.

In this video:
0:00 — Why existing tools force you to choose between power and ease
0:15 — Load your real code components, patterns, and templates into Portal
0:30 — Live demo: from template + Figma frame to a working prototype
1:10 — Real-time collaboration across designers, PMs, and reviewers
1:45 — Engineering handoff that continues the work instead of restarting it

What makes Portal different: No terminal. No Git. No local setup. Just describe what you want, and Portal builds it using your actual components and design rules. Not a generic approximation of them.
 
You're prototyping with production code in a cloud-based collaborative environment. When you're ready, share it with your team or customers with a single link. When engineers pick it up, they're extending a prototype built from the components they already use and maintain.
The gap between idea and shipped feature just got a lot smaller.
