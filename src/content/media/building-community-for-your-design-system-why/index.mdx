When we’re building our design systems for success, we usually look at the components and processes as the answer to good adoption. However, building community around your design system has been proven to be an essential ingredient to scaling a design system.

In this panel discussion, we’ll cover off why community building is integral to success, some of the tactics you can use to start on your community-building efforts, and open it up to your questions for our esteemed panel.
