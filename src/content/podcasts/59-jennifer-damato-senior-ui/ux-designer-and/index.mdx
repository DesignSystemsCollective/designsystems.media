Jen, Donnie and Chris discuss how design systems reflect the people a part of it, who use it and who maintain it; and the importance of collaboration, that systems are built for and with people.  
  
View the transcript of this episode, [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/18xf_wcK9g83_PJDLGgT55RfG6yMc8Go6/edit). 

**Guest**

Jen is a product designer and educator from NYC. She’s currently a Senior Experience Designer at Signet Jewelers, exploring ways to expand and improve their Amor Design System. You can find Jen on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jennifer-ip/) and [jennifer.damato.design](https://jennifer.damato.design/).  

Donnie is a design systems architect, adjunct faculty of UX curriculum, and armchair philosopher from New York. Author of [Gridless Design](https://gridless.design/). You can find Donnie on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fauxserious/) and [donnie.damato.design](https://donnie.damato.design/).
