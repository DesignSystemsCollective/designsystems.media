Curious about the fun parts of design systems? Check out this episode of the Design Systems Podcast featuring Davy Fung, co-host of design systems podcast [Design System Office Hours](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/design-system-office-hours/id1605819337),  who joined Chris to talk about working creatively within design system constraints, common structures for design systems, and building a Jedi Council of Design System Advocates. 

**Guest:** Davy Fung formerly led design systems across Disney+, Hulu, and Star+, now at Meta working on design systems for Meta Business Suite. You can find Davy on Twitter as @cobradave, his podcast [Design System Office Hours](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/design-system-office-hours/id1605819337), and on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/davidfung/).
